Cookie Policy

How Orbit Logic uses cookies and how you can manage your preferences.

Last updated: 1 January 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, to remember your preferences, and to provide information to website owners. Cookies cannot access other information on your device.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Necessary for the website to function properly. These enable core functions such as page navigation and cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

These help us understand how visitors interact with our website. We use Google Analytics to collect anonymised, aggregated data about page visits, time spent on site, and traffic sources. Google's privacy policy applies to data collected by Google Analytics.

Preference Cookies

These allow our website to remember choices you've made to provide enhanced, personalised features.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our site are placed by third-party services governed by their own policies. Third-party services we use include Google Analytics (analytics) and Netlify (hosting and form handling).

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings — most browsers allow you to view, block, or delete cookies. Note that restricting cookies may impact your experience on our site.

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking, install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
